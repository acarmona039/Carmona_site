Materials and Commisions

This may sound like an ad, but to visualize my ideas, I use a number of equipment, and Ohuhu markers are one of them. They have grown to be a vital part of the way I create.
I often find myself grabbing for these markers to add quality, and deep color to my artwork because of their amazing and changeable dual tips.
Ohuhu markers constantly provide the ability and quality I need to properly bring my ideas, whether I'm sketching precise details or mixing gradients...
I also frequently use ordinary lead pencils as the starting point for my creative projects. Their simple appearance hides their adaptability and reliability,
